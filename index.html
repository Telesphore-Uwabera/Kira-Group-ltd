Professional Development / Capacity Development

Would you like to see your employees perform better? Behave better and be more productive? We offer customized professional development services tailored to help your employees upgrade their skillset, better understand what they do, communicate more effectively and acquire more skills and competencies they need to work well. We work with you to design suitable programs for you and your team members. We are the solutions to the developing companies.

Monitoring and Evaluation / Impact Assessment

Work with us to evaluate the impact of your work, understand how you can do better. We also help you have your impact assessment in front of your eyes. They are are processes commonly used in various fields, such as development projects, programs, policies, and interventions, to assess the effectiveness, efficiency, and outcomes of these initiatives.These processes help organizations and stakeholders understand if their efforts are achieving their intended goals.

Program Design, Implementation and Monitoring

We help you understand how to plan, implement and evaluate the new programs. Effective program design, implementation, and monitoring work together to ensure that a program achieves its intended impact. Regular monitoring allows for adjustments to be made in real time, increasing the likelihood of success. These phases are cyclical, as lessons learned from monitoring can feed back into program design and implementation for continuous improvement.
